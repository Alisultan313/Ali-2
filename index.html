English Kurdish (Sorani)
The Last Testament of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) was conveyed on the night of his passing, and it stands as the sole testament within the extensive body of Islamic literature. دوا پەیمانی پێغەمبەر محەمەد (صلی الله علیه وسلم) لە شەوی کۆچی دواییدا گەیاندراوە و وەک تاکە پەیمان لەناو جەستەی بەرفراوانی ئەدەبیاتی ئیسلامیدا وەستاوە.
An Obligation Upon Muslims
“It is prescribed for you when death approaches [any] one of you if he leaves anything valuable [is that he should leave] a Will for the parents and near relatives according to what is acceptable – a duty upon the righteous.”
– Quran 2:180
